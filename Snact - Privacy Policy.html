Privacy Policy

calendar_month Last Updated: January 2026

Snact is designed to help you set reminders for screenshots, images, snaps, and voice notes. Your privacy is important to us, and this policy explains how your data is handled.

folder_open Information We Collect

Snact collects only what is required to provide reminder functionality.

This may include:

  • circle Images, screenshots, or voice notes you choose to save as reminders
  • circle Reminder timing and notification preferences
  • circle Basic app usage data for improving performance

Snact does not collect unnecessary personal or sensitive information.

engineering How Your Data Is Used

Your data is used only to:

  • image Display
    Display your saved screenshots, images, or voice notes.
  • notifications Trigger
    Trigger reminders at the scheduled time.
  • speed Improve
    Improve app reliability and user experience.

Your data is never sold or shared for advertising purposes.

lock Data Storage & Security

Your reminder data is stored securely on your device and/or secure cloud services when required for app functionality.

We use standard security practices to protect your data from unauthorized access.

hub Third-Party Services

Snact may use limited third-party services (such as notification delivery or crash analytics) strictly to operate and improve the app. These services are bound by their respective privacy policies.

check_box User Control

You are always in control:

  • delete Delete any screenshot, snap, or voice note reminder at any time
  • cancel Uninstall the app to stop all data collection

child_care Children's Privacy

Snact is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.
